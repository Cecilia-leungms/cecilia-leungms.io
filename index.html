We are Art Tale

A NGO for people to find their voice through art.

Why choose us #1Build Up Your Confidence

Not everyone is born with an inbuilt sense of self-confidence. Sometimes it can be hard to develop confidence. But, Participating in a form of art can help you at every stage of life and art is a great way for you to discover your value.

Why choose us #2Make Connection with Others

There’s a message here that resonates with anyone who creates something to share with others, be it visual art, performance, writing, or music. Giving away free content was about the value of the music becoming the connection itself.

Why choose Us #3Be Yourself

Unleashing your artistic side can open the mind to new and compelling ideas and engender discovery of passions and intellect. Thus, finding ways to be more creative, through art and science, will enrich the soul and provide inspiration for future ventures.

Why choose Us #4Skills

An education that includes the arts is essential to a human’s success in developing a fulfilling life. Skills developed through participation in the arts are increasingly important in the workplace and therefore, key to a successful career.

Our Classes

Three of the basic classes we created for you specifically.

Value & Contribution ClassFind Who You Are

Students will find their voice and self-confidence as they learn to create and as they learn of their role and abilities as change agents and are shown the impact they can have

Create & Power ClassBuild Up Your Skills
